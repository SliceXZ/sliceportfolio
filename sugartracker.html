SugarTracker

Category: Final Class Project

Tools: HTML/CSS, jQuery, PHP, AJAX, mySQL

Team members: Team of 2

Sugartracker is a website based application, which stores a database of sugar based drinks and exercises to burn the calories from the drinks off. This web application can be accessed by both "visitors", who can browse information in the site, and "users", who can create an account to track their personal consumption. The creation of this web application required front-end and back-end development and design.

Demo video: created by me

The Problem

Sugary drinks have been a major cause for diabetes and other disease. They are cheap, easily available and a lot of sugar can be consumed in just a few gulps. They have become more accesible than water itself in some areas, but there is a lack of awareness of the quantity of sugar in these drinks, and a way to track personal consumption.

Initial Considerations

To remedy this problem, we brainstormed a solution. We thought if people could be more aware of these problems through personal tracking, awareness would increase, consumption would decrease, thus reducing potential health risks associated with sugar consumption.
